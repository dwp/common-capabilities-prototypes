You should have a 12 digit Unique Property Reference Number (UPRN) for the address.



About this service

This UPRN service can be used to search & find locations in Great Britain with an address.

This UPRN lookup searches government databasese for an address, including the Valuations Office Agency and Ordnance Survey.

This service tries to ensure we have a consistent identifier for a property used in applications accross benefits.

It is not the same as your Council Tax reference number.
