Before you start

You should gather the following information about your business or domain to be procced:


Business(service) name:
Name of the your business or benefit to be used to configure in DWP Manage task service.

Domain
The domain where your business or benefit service resides. Your BA, Developers or Architects may have this information.

List of attributes
These are unique property or properities that are used in your business or benefit to define tasks and for allocating tasks to your agents.

Attributes are used to match tasks to agents. For example, agent roles or location or grade.

List of agents and their attributes:
This would be list of agents you want to give access to and the attributes for each agent. For example, Agent 'John Smith' can have attributes like 'decision maker, newcastle, level 7 clearance'.

For creating task definitions (task matching templates):

  • You should know the type of task and what attributes the task should have for.
  • Exection URL: Where the task is going to get executed
  • Priority of the task
  • SLA of the task
  • The name of the task that your agents understand. Your user researchers and content designers may have this information.


You will be able to come back to the setup task list at any time using services page.
