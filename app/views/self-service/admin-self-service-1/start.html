Before you start

To set up your business or service, you'll need to provide the following information:


Business or service name

The name of your business or benefit to be used to configure in DWP Manage task service.

Domain

The domain where your business or benefit service resides. Your BA, Developers or Architects may have this information.

List of attributes

These are unique property or properities that are used in your business or benefit to define tasks and for allocating tasks to your agents.

Attributes are used to match tasks to agents. For example, agent roles or location or grade.

List of agents and their attributes

This would be list of agents you want to give access to and the attributes for each agent.

For example, agent 'John Smith' can have attributes like

  • decision maker
  • Newcastle
  • level 7 clearance'

Task definitions (task matching templates)

To create task definitions (task matching templates) you need to know the:

  • type of task and what attributes the task should have for
  • exection URL: where the task is going to get executed
  • priority of the task
  • SLA of the task
  • name of the task that your agents understand. Your user researchers and content designers may have this information


You will be able to come back to the setup task list at any time using services page.
